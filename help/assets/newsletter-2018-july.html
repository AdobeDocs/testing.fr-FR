Tips & Tricks: Leverage Response Tokens to output Target-specific information to integrate with 3rd-party systems
 
Response tokens let you automatically output information specific to Adobe Target such as campaign details, user profile information, and geo information. You can use that information for debugging or integrating with 3rd-party systems such as Clicktale. Parameters you may use in Response Tokens include built-in profiles, activity information, geo information, or any information collected by your profile scripts.
