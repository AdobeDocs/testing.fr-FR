Tips & Tricks: Integrate Third-party data with the new dataProviders feature
 
dataProviders is a capability added in at.js 1.3 that allows you to easily pass data from third parties to Adobe Target. A third party could be a weather service, a DMP, or even your own web service. You can then use this data to build audiences, target content, and enrich the visitor profile.
